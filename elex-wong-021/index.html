Who are we?

Venture capital (VC) has continuously been gaining popularity in Europe, America and Asia, helping with the incubation of countless startups and turning them into multinational giants. VC has also contributed much to the rapid development of countries and regions. However, the term "venture" is rarely heard of here in the Philippines. But we believe that it is only a matter of time before the Internet era completely enters countries all over the world within the next five years, and the Philippines is no exception. Therefore, with the creation of the incubator named 021 Maker Space, we will turn ventures “from zero to one”.


021 Maker Space has formed alliances with a number of international venture capital groups and major consortiums. Taking on the form of a shared co-working space that will take shape with data model, 021 Maker Space will regularly hold "investor meetings" to incubate promising companies.

Why Choose Us

  5. Regular events: such as investor meetings and top venture capital training.
  6. Onsite receptionist and IT support to enhance your business experience.
  7. Providing professional and excellent office environment.
  8. Sharing of resources and connections.
